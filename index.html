We are HOSA,
A Health Careers Club at the Early College at Guilford

HOSA stands for Health Occupation Students of America. Here, at ECG, we compete in the annual North Carolina HOSA State Leadership Conference. As a health and medical-focused organization, we are also passionate about our service to the community.
