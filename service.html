Past Service Events

Because club members are interested in the medical field, HOSA typically hosts one health-related service event per month. However, because of the ongoing pandemic, we have cancelled theses events for the 2020-2021 school year. We hope to pick them up again in the future.

Here are some of our previous service events:

  • Chick-Fil-A Fundraiser with St. Jude's Comprehensive Cancer Center
  • Bone Marrow Drive
  • EMS Ride Along
  • STEAM Fair at Jefferson Elementary School
  • Pride Booth in Downtown Greensboro
